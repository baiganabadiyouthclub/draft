Welcome to our Youth Club

Our main focus to lift the public from the courses of poverty, hunger, illiteracy and diseases by starting and carrying out concentrated and intensive programs. We promote charitable values, literature, science, art, education and culture.

We celebrate national and international days and conduct sports, yoga class event in summer days. We celebrate national festivals, freedom fighters/ leaders birthdays, state festivals and other common festivals.

Aims and Objectives

To follow, without halt, one aim: that's the secret of success.

( Anna Pavlova )

  1. To start, manage and administer any Orphanage, Old age Homes, and Hospitals, libraries, Diagnostic Centers, Reading rooms, Schools, colleges, ITI institutes, Research Institutions and other institution and extend its benefits to society at large irrespective of caste, creed or social status.
  2. To support and promote the growing of trees and drilling of bore wells, restoration of water tanks, taking up drinking water projects, water development programs.
  3. To start and run rehabilitation centers for mentally retarded, physically handicapped persons and to start vocational centers.
  4. To lift the public from the curses of poverty, hunger, illiteracy and diseases by starting and carrying out concentrated and intensive programs.
  5. To promote cultural and artistic subjects / works like Drawings, paintings, music, shows dramatic performances and dances reflecting / exhibiting the preaching of all religions heads of India, and Indian cultures / traditions.
  6. To develop and mobilize Community and Natural Resources to give the right direction to people of the marginalized and economically weaker section of the Society for their sustainable overall development.
  7. To organize and take up Health, Educational and Welfare programs for needy Women and children on priority basis.
  8. To promote charitable values, literature, science, art, education and culture.
  9. To arrange / establish and maintain centers of adult education, vocational education technical training, low cost education, formal & non-formal education.
  10. To fight against HIV/AIDS and other communicable diseases, To aware people about various social issues to fight against evils, To organize and take-up Health, Educational and Welfare programmes for needy & poor, To promote and protect Indian Traditional music, dance, handicraft, handlooms, Art, modern art & literature.
  11. To establish centre(s) for the promotion of Music, Dance and other Arts, To work for Environmental Protection, Forestation, Plantation, Waste Land Management.
  12. To organize vocational training for women and needy people, To organize awareness camps, health camps, March, workshops, campaign and exhibition, To provide various medical services such as organizing health and awareness camps of Immunization, Eye Care, Motherhood, Child Care, and Communicable Diseases.
  13. To Establish and Manage Cultural Institutions, To Celebrate of National and international days and conduct Sports, yoga class event in summer days.
  14. To Celebrate National Festivals, Freedom Fighters / Leaders Birthdays, State Festival and other common festivals.
  15. To create awareness among the people on swachhata, water sanitation, control of diarrhea disease, child immunization, maternal care, child nutrition, HIV (AIDS) Anti drugs, gender justice, Environmental protection water conservation, no single use of plastic protection of wild animal use of organic fertilizer, use of solar energy etc.
  16. To provide consultancy services particularly rural unemployment youth, relating entrepreneurship, opportunities, skill carrier pathways for educative youth and society
  17. To Utilize government and non-government funds properly.
