About Us

Baiganabadi Youth Club - Growing Stronger Together

Welcome to our Youth Club

Our main focus to lift the public from the courses of poverty, hunger, illiteracy and diseases by starting and carrying out concentrated and intensive programs. We promote charitable values, literature, science, art, education and culture.

We celebrate national and international days and conduct sports, yoga class event in summer days. We celebrate national festivals, freedom fighters/ leaders birthdays, state festivals and other common festivals.
